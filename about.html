Who We Are

The Basotho Youth Festival is a yearly event created to support youth talent, culture and community development in Lesotho. It serves as a platform where young people can showcase their abilities,connect with others, and gain exposure in dfferent creative and proffessional field
RThe festival brings together a variety o activities that appeal to diverse interest. If features live music performances where upcoming and established artists entertain audiences and promote local music culture.
In addition, there are sports activities and competitions that encourage physical fitness,teamwork,and healthy lifestyle among the youth

The festival includes live music, sports, fashion, cultural performances and networking opportunities.
